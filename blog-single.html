Let’s Talk About XMU

by Xiamen University Official Website / Design

Xiamen University (XMU) was founded in 1921 by Mr. Chen Jiageng, a famous patriotic overseas Chinese leader. It is the first university founded by overseas Chinese in China's modern education history. It is also a high-level university focused on the national "211 Project" and "985 Project". In 2017, Xiamen University was selected into the list of class a world-class universities published by the state.

Founded in 1921 by Mr. Chen Jiageng, a patriotic overseas Chinese leader, Xiamen University is the first university founded by overseas Chinese in China's modern educational history, one of the earliest universities in China to recruit postgraduates, and the first university to build an independent campus overseas. Its early buildings are listed in the national key cultural protection units and the list of "the first batch of China's architectural heritage in the 20th century", known as "the power of the South" "The most beautiful university in China".
